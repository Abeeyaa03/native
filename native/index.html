Our Heritage

A Legacy of 1000 Years

Srimushnam is one of the eight Swayam Vyakta Kshetras of Lord Vishnu.

The Bhu Varaha Swamy Temple stands as a testament to the Chola and Vijayanagara architectural brilliance. Inscriptions dating back to the 11th century narrate tales of devotion and royal patronage.

Geographically, the village lies in the fertile Vellar River basin, blessing the land with lush agriculture and a serene climate.

Celebration of Unity

Vaikasi Ther Festival

Witness the grandeur of the 2nd largest temple chariot in Tamil Nadu.

This festival is a unique symbol of communal harmony where people of all faiths participate in pulling the chariot. The air fills with chants, music, and devotion.
